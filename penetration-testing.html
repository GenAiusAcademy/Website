Standard Penetration Testing Services

GenAius Academy offers professional penetration testing led by a qualified Ethical Hacker and Computer Science Teacher who has served at Dar Al Marefa Private School in Dubai. We help organisations understand and reduce their cyber risk.

Led by a Qualified Ethical Hacker

Penetration tests at GenAius are conducted by FM Charlie Storey, who holds a degree in Informatics, Computer Forensics & Ethical Hacking and has hands-on experience teaching Cybersecurity and Computer Science. That means your reports are both technically solid and clearly explained.

We focus on small to mid-size environments where a practical, human-readable report and clear remediation guidance are more valuable than jargon or unnecessary complexity.

What We Test

  • Small business and school networks
  • Web applications and portals
  • Exposed infrastructure and remote access
  • Misconfigurations and poor access controls
  • Password and credential weaknesses

Every engagement ends with a prioritised list of issues and clear, step-by-step mitigation advice.

Engagement Structure & Pricing

A typical standard penetration test runs between 2 and 4 days depending on scope. Pricing guidelines:

  • AED 1,500 per hour for small, focused tests
  • From AED 8,000 for a 2-day engagement
  • From AED 18,000 for a 4-day engagement

Final pricing depends on the number of systems, complexity and reporting requirements. For schools and charities, limited discounted rates may be available.
