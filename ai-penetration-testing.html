AI Penetration Testing & Red Teaming

GenAius offers AI penetration testing for organisations deploying AI tools, LLMs or AI-driven features. As a qualified Ethical Hacker and Computer Science Teacher with AI teaching experience, Charlie Storey helps you identify both technical and human risks around AI use.

What We Look For

  • Prompt injection and jailbreak attempts
  • Leaking confidential or sensitive information via AI
  • Model misuse or unsafe instructions
  • Poor access controls around AI tools
  • Unsafe automation built on top of AI outputs

We structure tests around realistic adversarial behaviour, then provide clear recommendations for technical and policy-level controls.

Who This Is For

AI Penetration Testing is ideal for:

  • Schools deploying AI tools to staff and students
  • Companies integrating AI chatbots into websites
  • Teams building internal AI assistants
  • Consultancies who want independent validation

The goal is to make AI usage safer, clearer and aligned with your safeguarding and compliance obligations.
